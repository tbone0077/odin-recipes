Pepperoni Bread

Ingredients

  • 1 pkg or 2.5 tsp rapid-rise yeast
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • garlic powder
  • oregano
  • basil
  • seasoned pepper
  • red pepper flakes
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/3 cup dry milk
  • shredded mozzarella cheese
  • large sliced pepperoni

Steps

  1. Dissolve yeast, sugar, salt in warm water with olive oil
  2. Add herbs to your liking - garlic powder, oregano, basil, seasoned pepper, red pepper flakes
  3. Add flour and dry milk
  4. Knead dough - adding additional flour if needed until no longer sticky
  5. Lightly coat dough with olive oil
  6. Place in bowl-cover to rise ~30 mins
  7. Spread dough on greased cookie sheet
  8. Layer the mozzarella cheese and pepperoni
  9. Roll into a tight log
  10. Let rise again ~30 mins
  11. Bake @ 350 degrees for 25-30 minutes
