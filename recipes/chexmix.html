Chex Mix

yummy chex mix

This recipe will make your mouth water!

Ingredients

  • 6 tbsps butter
  • 2 tbsps worcestershire sauce
  • 1.5 tsp seasoned salt
  • .75 tsp onion powder
  • .75 tsp garlic powder
  • 3 cups corn chex cereal
  • 3 cups rice chex cereal
  • 3 cups wheat chex cereal
  • 1 cup quaker oatmeal squares
  • 1 cup sesame sticks
  • 1 cup mixed nuts
  • 1 cup pretzels
  • 1 cup broken garlic chips

Steps

  1. Melt butter and add seasonings. Pour over cereal mix
  2. Heat oven to 250 degrees
  3. Bake for one hour, stirring every 15 minutes
  4. optional - sprinkle additional garlic powder and worcestershire sauce at each stirring
