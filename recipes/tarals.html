Tarals

Ingredients

  • makes approx. 300
  • 4 tbsp salt
  • 5 tbsp black pepper
  • 5 lbs all purpose flour
  • 1 lb self-rising flour
  • 1 lb melted butter
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 6 eggs (beaten)
  • 1.5 - 2.5 cups water

Steps

  1. Mix together salt, black pepper, all purpose flour, self-rising flour in large bowl
  2. Make hole in the dry ingredients and add pool of moist ingredients
  3. Gently mix in water
  4. Mix and knead all together and form 3-5 smooth logs
  5. Let logs sit half an hour covered with a damp towel
  6. Have a drink... or two
  7. Cut logs into small pieces for rolling
  8. Roll into tarals and place on towels to dry
  9. Drop tarals into boiling water until they float
  10. Scoop out with straining spoon and place on towels to dry
  11. Bake @ 350 degrees (~35 mins) until they curl and brown
  12. Place directly into brown paper bag
